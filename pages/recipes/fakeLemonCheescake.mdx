# Fake Lemon Cheesecake

| Serves | Preparation Time | Cooking Time |
|--------|------------------|--------------|
| 12     | TBD              | TBD          |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient                 | Amount |
|----------------------------|--------|
| Digestive biscuits         | 250 g  |
| Butter                     | 150 g  |
| Lemons - zest and juice    | 6      |
| Condensed milk - sweetened | 400 g  |
| Double cream               | 350 ml |

## Instructions

1. Crush the digestive biscuits in a plastic bag with a rolling pin or in a food processor.
2. Gently melt the butter in a pan.
3. Mix the crushed biscuits with the melted butter until well combined.
4. Cover the base of a 26 cm pie dish or a loose-bottomed tin with the biscuit mixture and press it firmly down with the back of a spoon.
5. Chill the base in the fridge while preparing the filling.
6. In a bowl, mix the condensed milk, lemon zest, and lemon juice until well combined.
7. In a separate bowl, whip the double cream until it forms stiff peaks.
8. Gently fold the whipped cream into the lemon mixture until fully incorporated.
9. Pour the lemon mixture onto the chilled biscuit base and spread it evenly.
10. Chill the pie in the fridge for at least 3 hours or until set before serving.
