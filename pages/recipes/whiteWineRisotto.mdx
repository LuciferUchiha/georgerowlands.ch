# White Wine Risotto

## Ingredients

| Ingredient      | Amount |
|-----------------|--------|
| Onion           | 1      |
| Garlic clove    | 1      |
| Risotto rice    | 1 cup  |
| White wine      | 125 ml |
| Water           | 5 dl   |
| Vegetable stock | 1 tsp  |

## Instructions

1. Finely chop the onion and garlic.
2. Add the garlic and onion to a pan and cook until the onion is translucent.
3. Add the white wine and risotto and stir.
4. Dissolve the stock in the boiling water and pour over the risotto.
5. Leave it all on a low simmer until cooked while stirring it every few minutes.

## Tips

- Try adding 150 g of prawns per person towards the end of the cooking time. You can also cook about 150 g of scallops per person to go with it.
- Cook about 150 g of scallops per person to go with it.
- Top your finished risotto with some smoked salmon.
- Try adding 1 leek cut into strips about halfway through the cooking process.
- To make your risotto even creamier, add 1 tbsp of crème fraîche or grated cheese at the end.
- Squeeze some lemon or lime juice over the risotto at the end.
