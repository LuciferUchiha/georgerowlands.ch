# Curry Yogurt Chicken

| Serves | Preparation Time | Cooking Time |
|--------|------------------|--------------|
| 2      | TBD              | TBD          |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient                          | Amount  |
|-------------------------------------|---------|
| Chicken thighs                      | 4       |
| Greek yogurt - plain nonfat         | 250 ml  |
| Lemons - zest and juice             | 1       |
| Garlic cloves - chopped             | 2       |
| Oil - avocado or extra virgin olive | 2 tbsp  |
| Sriracha                            | 2 tbsp  |
| Coriander - fresh and chopped       | 1/3 cup |
| Salt                                | 1 tsp   |
| Cumin                               | 1 tbsp  |
| Curry powder                        | 1 tbsp  |
| Turmeric                            | 1 tbsp  |

## Instructions
1. In a large bowl, whisk together all the ingredient apart from the chicken.
2. Score the chicken thighs and add them to the marinade, making sure they are coated well.
3. Transfer the chicken and marinade to a ziplock bag, seal it, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
4. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
5. Arrange the chicken on a baking tray and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is golden brown.
6. Serve hot with your choice of side dish.
