## Ingredients

| Ingredient      | Amount   |
|-----------------|----------|
| Pasta           | 2 people |
| Butter          | 100g     |
| Full cream      | 300 ml   |
| Garlic cloves   | 2        |
| Salt            | 1tsp     |
| Pepper          | 1tsp     |
| Parmesan cheese | 400g     |

## Instructions

1. Finely chop the garlic.
2. In a large pan, add the butter and cream and simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
3. Whisk in the garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper and cook for 1 minute.
4. Gradually whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce is smooth.
5. Serve with pasta of choice.

## Tips

- Cook some chicken separately and add it back to the sauce at the end.
- If the sauce is too thick, thin it out by adding a splash of milk or cream.
- Garnish with chopped fresh herbs, such as parsley or basil, for added freshness and color.
